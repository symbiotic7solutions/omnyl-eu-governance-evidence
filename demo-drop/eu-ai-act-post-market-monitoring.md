# EU AI Act Post-Market Monitoring Evidence

This document is the live demo drop Morgan adds to the Omnyl EU evidence repo.

Post-market monitoring owner:

- Accountable owner: Head of Governance.
- Operational owner: Support Operations Lead.
- Reviewer: Data Protection Officer.

Monitoring cadence:

- Weekly support triage sample of at least 30 AI-assisted records.
- Monthly trend review covering false positive, false negative, unresolved escalation, and customer correction rates.
- Quarterly management review covering residual risk, incidents, and corrective action closure.

Escalation thresholds:

- False classification rate above 5 percent triggers a corrective action.
- Any customer-impacting unsupported AI recommendation triggers incident review.
- Any new high-risk indicator triggers legal and governance review before release.

Corrective action record:

- Owner, due date, evidence required, validation step, and closure approval are required.
- Closure evidence is linked back to the audit evidence repository.

Retest trigger:

- Adding this file to the evidence repository requires FlowStack to ingest the new evidence, create a new dated assessment run, and update the active governance valuation while preserving the previous runs.
