# AI Use Principles

Omnyl EU uses AI tools to support product analytics, customer support triage, and internal knowledge retrieval.

Principles:

- Use AI to assist staff, not to replace accountable owners.
- Avoid using AI outputs for automated legal, financial, employment, or eligibility decisions.
- Escalate uncertain outputs to a human reviewer.
- Record material incidents in the company issue tracker.

Known gaps:

- No approved ISO/IEC 42001 AI management system scope.
- No EU AI Act risk classification register.
- No documented human oversight procedure by system.
- No post-market monitoring cadence or owner.
